01. Asteroids

This project, developed during my first academic year, is a faithful reimplementation of the classic Asteroids arcade game originally created by Atari. It was entirely programmed in C, utilizing a proprietary internal graphics library provided by the school.

The goal was to replicate the gameplay and aesthetic of the original, including vector-style graphics, physics-based movement, collision handling, and scoring system, while learning the fundamentals of low-level programming and graphical rendering.
