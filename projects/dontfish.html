01. Don't Fish Me

Don't Fish Me is a frantic cooperative platformer created in just 48 hours for the Global Game Jam 2024. Our team of six split responsibilities across design, art, audio, and code.

The game loves to mess with its racers: each stage throws surprise harpoons, slippery currents, and moving walls that seem designed just to stop your run. Clearing a level means coordinating boosts, timing respawns, and working as a squad so at least one fish can touch the buoy.
