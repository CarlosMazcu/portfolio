01. PhyloGen

PhyloGen is a collaborative procedural botany playground. At its core lies an L-System interpreter that grows plants by rewriting symbols into turtles, which then trace vector branches, leaves, and blossoms before being lofted into 3D meshes.

The editor exposes dozens of knobs letting you morph a seedling into a dense forest or coral-like abstractions. A live preview updates every tweak, while export profiles bake the geometry into optimized meshes ready for game engines.
