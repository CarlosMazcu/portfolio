01. PhyloGen

PhyloGen is a vegetation generator developed as a collaborative project together with Marc Folgado. Based on L-Systems, it produces 2D representations using vector graphics, which are later transformed into 3D meshes.

The program offers a wide range of settings to define rules, angles, iterations, and growth parameters, enabling the procedural creation of trees, shrubs, and other botanical structures.
