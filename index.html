How I Work

I specialize in gameplay and graphics, building robust and efficient systems in C++, Unreal Engine, and OpenGL. From rendering pipelines to internal tools, I like taking features from technical design to final polish.

But code is only one part. My teammates often say I'm the one who keeps the good vibe in the team. I believe communicating well and adapting to different ways of working is just as important as writing clean code. I'm comfortable working under pressure, and I enjoy that environment where everyone rows in the same direction to move a project forward.

I also code outside of work. I'm always tinkering with a side project or learning some new technology out of pure curiosity. After work hours, I keep coding because I enjoy it, whether that's personal projects or learning new things.
